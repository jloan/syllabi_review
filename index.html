Why Inquire Into Syllabi?

  • central to our teaching
  • reveals our assumptions, attitudes, values
    and beliefs
  • a site to make equity-minded work explicit
  • a site we can immediately change
"[S]yllabus review is about creating more equity-minded syllabi, [but] it is also about critically and systematically reflecting on your teaching, as well as learning how to make inquiry a key, routine aspect of your practice." -- CUE

What Does an Equity-Minded Syllabus Do?

  1. Demystifies[Fairness]
  2. Welcomes[Care]
  3. Validates[Care]
  4. Partners[Care]
  5. Represents[Transformation]
  6. Deconstructs[Transformation]
